FOUNDERS' COHORT

As A Founder You Will:

  • Be recognized as a Campus Entrepreneur
  • Recruit a team of 2-5 members for Startup pitch
  • Attend monthly Young Ambitious Ones workshops
  • Improve your entrepreneur pitch monthly
  • Attend entrepreneur panel
  • Attend Networking Events
  • Recruit peers onto Startup Tree
  • Earn Entrepreneurship Certifications and Badges
  • The Cohort will run virtually from Feb - May, 2022

OUR PARTNERS

Young Ambititious One (YAO), is a Learning and Career Development Platform dedicated to empowering students, for success in business, technology, and entrepreneurship. YAO provides interactice professional and career development programs, career advisory, and access to entrepreneurial opportunities.

TalentLMS is an online training platform that offers a series of short, fun and interactive modules focused on entrepreneurial skill development. The modules will help students immerse themselves in the world of entrepreneurship - even if they don't have a business idea or startup.

Startup Tree continuously provides opportunities to students' year-round to a network of over 100 institutions and directors committed to pursuing entrepreneurial collaborations and connections.

Uvii is an education software company providing access to learning and evaluation on mobile devices. Uvii allows us to stand up surveys quickly and take advantage of action command messaging.
